Established in May 1999 by famous and religious schlor Maulana Bashir Ahmed Farooqui, Saylani Welfare International Trust was built on the fundamentals of breaking the cycle of poverty, alleviating the finencial troubles of the poor, giving people a chance to live a dignified life and spreading happiness.
We are an organisation tat believes in lighting up the lives of underprivilged people across the world.
